# Gemini Integration Notes

This project was developed with the assistance of Antigravity (Gemini). Here are the key integration points and design decisions made during the process.

## Design Philosophy
The AI Portal follows a "Premium Dark" aesthetic, prioritizing visual excellence and smooth user experience. We used:
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur effects for a modern feel.
- **Micro-animations**: Transition and hover effects for interactivity.
- **Local-First Architecture**: Ensuring privacy and speed by using local databases and LLMs.

## Future Gemini Enhancements
- **Agentic Workflows**: Integrating Gemini-based tools to automate data indexing.
- **Deep Research**: Connecting the portal to Gemini for automated AI research summaries.
